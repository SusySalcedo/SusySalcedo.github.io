Graphic Design

GRAPHICS

I have always enjoyed the art of creating graphics and been facinated with learning about Typography. Taking several graphic design courses at Texas Christian University with a few internships under my belt I was able to fuel that creativity. Interning at Social Factor I had the chance to create various graphics using Adobe Software for different accounts for either special events or weekly campaigns.





Intrepid Fallen Heroes Fund

I created graphics for Intrepid Fallen Heroes Fund First Giving Day, December 1, 2015 to help raise money in order to build treatment centers for our service members. I updated the graphic to show the progression of money being raised, soon reaching the final goal. These graphics were posted on this account's Social Media platforms throughout the day in order for followers to have a visual of the donations accumulating. I used Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop.






Agilysys

Designed holiday e-Mail graphic for Agilysys, a hospitality software and solutions company. Adobe PhotoShop, Illustrator, InDesign.




Hawaiian Falls

Hawaiian Falls Waterparks had a weekly posting trend of posting words in Hawaiian in correlation to the season. This graphic was made before Thanksgiving and posted on several social media platforms for specific Hawaiian Falls locations. Adobe Illustrator and PhotoShop.
