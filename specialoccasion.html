Special Occasion Designs

Wedding Logos

Why not make that special day even more special with a personalized wedding logo?





Pollock Wedding 2017

I included a clover in a modern coat of arms style logo to represent the Irish roots of the groom and an Eiffel Tower to symbolize the couple's proposal location. Creating a personalized wedding logo led to its many uses. I hand made coffee stirrers which included one side of the logo and one side with the couple's picture which were set out for guests at a coffee bar. The same logo was also printed onto sticker decals that were placed on small bags filled with rose petals for the couple's exit at the end of the night. I enjoyed making something that included a deeper meaning than just initials by strategically placing different symbols.
